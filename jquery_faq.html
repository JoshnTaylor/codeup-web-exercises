Where is the park?
Good question, it's over the river and through the woods!
How do I get there?
By car! By boat! Or by Plane!
When is the park open?
Monday thru Friday, 8 AM to 8 PM!
Are dogs allowed at your park?
Absolutely!
Are cats allowed at your park?
All animals are allowed on our park property!
How long is your local hiking trail?
8 miles, with built in amenities along the path!
Is there an entry fee?
Yes, to keep our parks up to date and cared for it will be 5 dollars per person.
Is there on site parking?
Yes! We have an abundance of parking spaces at the beginning of our trailhead.
Are bikes allowed on your park?
To protect our wildlife, bikes are not allowed on the paths.
Do you sell sun screen?
We sell sun screen and many other useful items you may have forgot at our gift shop!
